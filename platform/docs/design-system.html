CareOS Design System

The brand + UI system for CareOS by LaunchFlow (Business Intuitive Inc.) — an AI-native clinical operating system. Three deliberate surfaces: a loud marketing site, a dense dark EHR portal, and a warm patient portal. One accent, one typeface.

Three surfaces — deliberately different
Marketing · EHR portal · Patient portal
Audience: CIOs, clinics
Marketing site
Loud. Full-bleed color blocks. Oversized display type. Evidence-first.
Audience: Clinicians
EHR portal
Dark. Dense. Focused — a precision tool.
Audience: Patients
Patient portal
Light. Warm. Soft-shadowed. Reassuring.

One thread ties all three: lime (#c4ff4d) as the single accent and Space Grotesk as the display typeface.

Voice & Copy
Content principles
Confident, clinical, human. Numbers over adjectives.
✓ Write this
"47 min saved per shift"
"~30,000 admin actions/yr automated"
"Clinical work that works itself."
"HIPAA §164.312(b) hash-chained"
✕ Avoid this
"Empowering patients"
"Seamless care journey"
"Holistic wellness platform"
"Next-generation healthcare"
Casing rules
Sentence case — all headings and body copy
UPPERCASE — eyebrow labels only, with 0.16em tracking
Title Case — nav items and product names (CareOS, Patient Fishbowl™)
No emoji in product or marketing copy. ✓ → ™ ⚡ only in badges/bullets.
